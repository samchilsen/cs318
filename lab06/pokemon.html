Ampharos

Ampharos, as the chief of the Expedition Society, is primarily involved in investigating the recent events of Pokémon being turned into stone by an unknown power

Archen

He is a member of the Expedition Society and the specialist in sky expeditions, despite the fact that he cannot fly.

Bidoof

He is a member of Wigglytuff's Guild. He stars in his own Special Episode in Explorers of Sky, entitled Bidoof's Wish. He is also a recruitable Pokémon in Pokémon Super Mystery Dungeon.

Chatot

He is the head of intelligence of the guild and Wigglytuff's second-in-command, as well as his best friend. Chatot assigns explorations to the guild members, and is in charge when Wigglytuff falls asleep.

Celebii

Celebi is a Shiny Pokémon that appears in Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time and Explorers of Darkness and Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Sky.

Charizard

He originally appeared in the series when being summoned from a Poké Ball, and later became a playable character in Brawl, first as a part of the Pokémon Trainer's party, and later as an independent character in subsequent installments.
