About

I was born and raised in Manitowoc, Wisconsin and from an early age was always interested in the history of Manitowoc, specifically the submarine industry. My interest in history prompted me to declare a major in history when I enrolled at the University of Wisconsin – Manitowoc. When I left UW – Manitowoc to attend UW – Eau Claire, I decided to pursue both of my passions – history and political science. My love for politics led me into the political realm where I am today.

I believe strongly in the “work hard, play hard” mantra. While I enjoy my professional and educational endeavors, I also enjoy spending time with family and friends in my free time. I also enjoy golfing, watching Jeopardy, playing NBA 2K, and of course, reading about the submarine industry.

Please reach out to me using the contact information below if you would like to talk politics, submarines, or life – I look forward to speaking with you soon!
